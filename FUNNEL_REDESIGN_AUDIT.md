## Part 1: Shared Demo Line â€” High-Conversion Hook

### The Problem with the Current Page

The current landing page asks visitors to fill out a 4-field form and wait for setup *before* experiencing the product. This is a leap of faith for an AI voice product â€” users can't hear what they're buying.

### The Solution: Interactive Demo Line

A **shared demo phone number** that anyone can call instantly, no sign-up required. This:
- Eliminates friction (zero commitment to experience the product)
- Demonstrates value in 30 seconds
- Creates "aha moment" before the pitch
